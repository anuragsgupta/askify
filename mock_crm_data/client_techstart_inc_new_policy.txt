UPDATED REFUND POLICY - ALL ENTERPRISE CLIENTS
=================================================================

This policy supersedes all previous refund communications and applies 
to all Enterprise Plan customers effective January 1, 2024.

-------------------------------------------------------------------
ENHANCED MONEY-BACK GUARANTEE
-------------------------------------------------------------------

ELIGIBILITY PERIOD:
- Extended to 60 days (previously 30 days)
- Calculated from initial purchase date
- No usage restrictions (previously limited to <10 users)

REFUND AMOUNT:
- 100% FULL REFUND (increased from 50%)
- Includes all subscription fees paid
- Includes setup fees (previously non-refundable)
- Includes any add-on purchases

PROCESSING TIME:
- 5-7 business days (improved from 10-15 days)
- Automatic processing (no manual approval needed)
- Email confirmation sent within 24 hours

NO QUESTIONS ASKED:
- No reason required for refund request
- No usage data review
- No exit interview required
- Simplified one-click refund process

-------------------------------------------------------------------
WHAT HAPPENS AFTER REFUND
-------------------------------------------------------------------

ACCOUNT STATUS:
- Account remains active for 30 days after refund
- All features remain accessible during transition period
- Data export tools available for 90 days
- Option to reactivate within 90 days without setup fee

DATA RETENTION:
- All data retained for 90 days (previously 90 days)
- Extended to 180 days upon request
- Secure deletion after retention period
- Compliance with GDPR and data protection laws

REACTIVATION OPTIONS:
- No penalty for reactivation within 90 days
- Setup fee waived for returning customers
- Previous configuration restored automatically
- Special "welcome back" discount: 20% off first 3 months

-------------------------------------------------------------------
SPECIAL CASES
-------------------------------------------------------------------

PARTIAL REFUNDS:
- Available for annual subscriptions
- Prorated based on unused months
- Minimum refund: 3 months remaining

TECHNICAL ISSUES:
- Full refund if service unavailable >24 hours
- Automatic credit for downtime >4 hours
- No questions asked, automatic processing

DISSATISFACTION:
- Full refund regardless of usage
- Optional feedback survey (not required)
- Priority support for transition assistance

WHY WE MADE THESE CHANGES
-------------------------------------------------------------------

Customer Feedback:
- 78% of customers requested longer refund period
- 65% felt 50% refund was insufficient
- 82% wanted faster processing

Competitive Analysis:
- Industry standard is 60-day, 100% refund
- Our old policy was below market standard
- New policy positions us as industry leader

Business Impact:
- Reduced refund requests by 40% (more confidence)
- Increased trial-to-paid conversion by 35%
- Improved customer satisfaction scores by 50%
- Enhanced brand reputation and trust

HOW TO REQUEST A REFUND
-------------------------------------------------------------------

ONLINE PORTAL:
1. Log into your account dashboard
2. Navigate to Billing > Refund Request
3. Click "Request Full Refund" button
4. Receive confirmation email within 24 hours
5. Refund processed within 5-7 business days

EMAIL REQUEST:
- Send email to: refunds@company.com
- Subject: "Refund Request - [Account ID]"
- Include: Account email and reason (optional)
- Response within 24 hours

PHONE REQUEST:
- Call: (555) 123-4567
- Available: 24/7 (previously 9-5 only)
- Immediate processing
- Confirmation email sent
